Privacy and Cookies Policy

1. Information We Collect:

a. Personal Information: When you make a reservation, we collect personal information such as your name, contact details, and driver's license information.

b. Payment Information: To process payments, we collect billing details. However, we do not store credit card information after the transaction is complete.

c. Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access our website, including IP address, browser type, and operating system.

2. How We Use Your Information:

a. Reservation and Rental Services: We use your information to process reservations, manage rentals, and provide customer support.

b. Communication: We may use your contact information to send reservation confirmations, updates, and promotional offers.

c. Improving Our Services: We analyze data to enhance our website, services, and user experience.

3. Data Sharing:

a. Third-Party Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers to facilitate reservations, payments, and other services.

b. Legal Compliance: We may disclose your information to comply with legal obligations or respond to lawful requests.

4. Cookies:

a. What Are Cookies: Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us improve functionality and analyze usage.

b. Types of Cookies We Use: Essential Cookies: Necessary for website functionality, Analytical Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our site, and Marketing Cookies: Used for personalized advertising.

c. Managing Cookies: You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings.

5. Your Privacy Choices:

a. Opt-Out: You can opt-out of marketing communications by following the instructions in the emails we send.

b. Access and Correction: You have the right to access and correct your personal information. Contact us for assistance.

6. Security:

a. Security Measures: We implement security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is entirely secure.

7. Changes to this Policy:

a. Updates: We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices. The date at the top indicates the last revision.
